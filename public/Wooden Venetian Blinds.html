WOODEN VENETIAN BLINDS

The WOODEN VENETIAN BLIND Continues to be a high-end classic blind option, ideal for traditional and upscale interiors. It features solid timber slats that offer a distinct appearance and texture, highlighted by the natural grain variations of the wood.

  • Available in 50mm width slats only.
  • The slats are made of solid timber.
  • Being a natural product variations in colour, grain & texture are inherent to the product.
  • Our blinds meet International standards in UV stabilisation and colour fastness.
  • These blinds are made for light control, slight light penetration does occur between slats even when closed.
  • Blinds can be MOTORISED
