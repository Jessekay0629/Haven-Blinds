Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How do you order finished product?
We work on finished sizes. We do NOT make deductions. Our tolerance is 3mm in width and height. All dimensions are in mm.
Is there a warranty on your products?
Yes, absolutely there is. Please see our WARRANTY page for full details, but below is a quick reference list.
What warranty coverage do you provide?
All Windovert products come with comprehensive warranty coverage. Our standard warranty includes 10 years on materials, 5 years on installation workmanship, and lifetime support for hardware components. Extended warranty options are available for enhanced protection and peace of mind.
How often should I schedule maintenance services?
We recommend annual maintenance checks to ensure optimal performance and longevity of your windows and doors. Our maintenance service includes cleaning, lubrication, weatherstripping inspection, and hardware adjustment. Regular maintenance helps prevent issues and maintains your warranty coverage.
Are your products energy efficient?
Yes, all Windovert products are designed with energy efficiency in mind. Our windows feature double or triple-pane glazing, low-E coatings, and advanced framing materials that significantly reduce heat transfer. This helps lower energy costs and maintains comfortable indoor temperatures year-round.
Do you provide free estimates?
Absolutely! We provide complimentary on-site consultations and detailed estimates for all projects. Our experienced team will assess your needs, take precise measurements, and provide you with a comprehensive quote that includes all materials, labor, and timeline expectations.
What should I do if my window or door needs repair?
Contact our service department immediately for any repair needs. We offer emergency repair services and can typically schedule service calls within 24-48 hours. Our certified technicians carry common replacement parts and can resolve most issues during the initial visit.
How do I file a warranty claim?
Filing a warranty claim is simple. Contact our customer service team with your installation details and description of the issue. We'll schedule an inspection and, if covered under warranty, complete the necessary repairs or replacements at no cost to you. Keep your warranty documentation handy for faster processing.
